Pin functions
-------------

These are the default pin functions in Simba.

+-------------+--------+
|  Function   |  Pin   |
+=============+========+
|  UART0 TX   |  PA9   |
+-------------+--------+
|  UART0 RX   |  PA10  |
+-------------+--------+
|  UART1 TX   |  PA2   |
+-------------+--------+
|  UART1 RX   |  PA3   |
+-------------+--------+
|  UART2 TX   |  PB10  |
+-------------+--------+
|  UART2 RX   |  PB11  |
+-------------+--------+
|  SPI0 SCK   |  PA5   |
+-------------+--------+
|  SPI0 MISO  |  PA6   |
+-------------+--------+
|  SPI0 MOSI  |  PA7   |
+-------------+--------+
|  SPI1 SCK   |  PA13  |
+-------------+--------+
|  SPI1 MISO  |  PA14  |
+-------------+--------+
|  SPI1 MOSI  |  PA15  |
+-------------+--------+
|  SPI2 SCK   |  PC10  |
+-------------+--------+
|  SPI2 MISO  |  PC11  |
+-------------+--------+
|  SPI2 MOSI  |  PC12  |
+-------------+--------+
|  I2C0 SCL   |  PB8   |
+-------------+--------+
|  I2C0 SDA   |  PB9   |
+-------------+--------+
|  I2C1 SCL   |  PF0   |
+-------------+--------+
|  I2C1 SDA   |  PF1   |
+-------------+--------+
|  CAN TX     |  PD1   |
+-------------+--------+
|  CAN RX     |  PD0   |
+-------------+--------+
