Pin functions
-------------

These are the default pin functions in Simba.

+------+-------+--------+-------------+
| List | Index |  Pin   |  Function   |
+======+=======+========+=============+
|  X1  |   12  |   PB0  |    CAN TX   |
+------+-------+--------+-------------+
|  X2  |    9  |   PB1  |    CAN RX   |
+------+-------+--------+-------------+
|  X1  |   10  |   PB2  |  UART0 TX   |
+------+-------+--------+-------------+
|  X2  |   10  |   PB3  |  UART0 RX   |
+------+-------+--------+-------------+
|  X4  |    6  |   PC2  |   LED (D8)  |
+------+-------+--------+-------------+
